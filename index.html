About Us

TerraLuna is a family-owned incubator and professional maker space based in Livermore, California. Livermore is the home of two major national labs, offering a highly technical community nestled among the greenery of California's historic wine country. With a progressive, tech-oriented city government maintaining a small-town feel, Livermore is a relaxing escape from nearby Silicon Valley.

We provide co-manufacturing facilities and technology infrastructure for ventures ranging from micro-startups to the Fortune 500, and are pioneering the use of blockchain and blockmesh-based collaboration and distributed governance within and between member organizations.
